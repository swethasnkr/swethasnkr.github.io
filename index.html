I’m a PhD candidate at Johns Hopkins University exploring the origins, growth, and evolution of black holes over cosmic time using multiwavelength data from both ground and space-based instruments, with an emphasis on infrared wavelengths. I specifically focus on the universe’s brightest beacons—quasars—and the dynamic environments that shape them.
