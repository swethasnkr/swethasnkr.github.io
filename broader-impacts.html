My Outreach

PHA Mentorship Committee / PENGUIN Organizer

Strong mentorship has the power to shape careers, build confidence, and create lasting networks. I serve as an organizer for the JHU PHA Mentorship Program. If you're interested in joining, please email us at pha-mentorship-committee@live.johnshopkins.edu.

I’m also a co-founder of PENGUIN Mentoring, a cross-institutional initiative designed to connect mentees with mentors across career stages and research backgrounds.

Black (Hole) Coffee Journal Club

I co-founded Black (Hole) Coffee, a journal club that brings together graduate students, postdocs, and faculty to discuss the latest research on black holes—from observational results to theoretical models.

If you’d like to present or join our mailing list, please contact us at ssanka10@jh.edu or mkarmen1@jhu.edu.

MSDGC Observatory Fellow

As a Maryland Space Grant Consortium (MSDGC) Observatory Fellow, I lead public observing nights and support local STEM outreach and education. Join us at the Bloomberg Observatory to view planets, stars, and galaxies—weather permitting, we're typically open Fridays from 8–10 PM!

For details, visit the MSDGC Observatory Open House page.
