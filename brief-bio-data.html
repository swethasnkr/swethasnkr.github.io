Research Interests


  • Lyman Continuum leakage of massive, evolved galaxies.
  • Characterizing the spatially resolved momentum, energetics, outflow rates, and effects of AGN feedback and outflows
  • Emission line spectroscopy to trace gas properties; spatially resolved analyses using IFU and "IFU-like" data (Power of Slitless spectroscopy using JWST)
  • Star formation suppression and quenching in Early type galaxies due to low luminosity AGN activity (maintenance-mode feedback), and their weak "winds".
  • Using Lyman-alpha probe to understand high redshift galaxies and constraining known calibration relations up to high-redshift.
  • Temporal symmetry, acceleration, and decaying mechanism of Blazars (AGN jets pointed towards our line of sight).
  • Characterizing chromospheric spectral lines in Solar flares.
